Latest FAQ’s

Can I get a quotation for special cargo?

Yes, you can get a quotation for 20' Open Top, 40' Open Top, and 40' Open Top High Cube containers. Simply select the equipment type as “Open Top (in gauge)” to receive a quotation. Please note that rates will only be applicable for in-gauge cargo. For all other special cargo requests, please contact call us.

What are the charges that need to be settled at load port along with the freight?

All charges are either export- (origin), import- (destination), or sea-freight-related. Depending on the agreement between shipper and consignee, the charges either have to be prepaid or collected (there might also be legal requirements in some countries).
